# Kerbe Tech Test Data

This folder contains realistic business documents for testing the Kerbe AI analytics platform.

## Files Overview

### Financial Statements
- **kerbe_tech_balance_sheet_2024.csv** - Complete balance sheet with assets, liabilities, and equity
- **kerbe_tech_income_statement_2024.csv** - Income statement showing revenue, costs, and net loss
- **kerbe_tech_cash_flow_2024.csv** - Cash flow statement with operating, investing, and financing activities
- **kerbe_tech_annual_report_2024.txt** - Comprehensive annual report combining all financial data

### Business Data
- **kerbe_tech_products.csv** - Product catalog with 10 AI/tech products, pricing, and sales data
- **kerbe_tech_customers.csv** - Customer database with 10 enterprise clients across various industries
- **kerbe_tech_orders.csv** - Order history with 15 transactions showing sales patterns

## Expected Analysis Results

### Financial Health Indicators
- **Revenue**: $12,350,000 (strong growth)
- **Gross Profit**: $4,150,000 (33.6% margin)
- **Operating Loss**: ($200,000) (concerning)
- **Net Loss**: ($305,000) (needs attention)
- **Cash Position**: $2,500,000 (healthy)
- **Total Assets**: $19,450,000 (strong balance sheet)

### Key Insights the AI Should Identify
1. **Revenue Growth**: Strong revenue of $12.35M indicates good market demand
2. **Profitability Issues**: Operating loss suggests cost management problems
3. **High Operating Expenses**: $4.35M in operating expenses vs $4.15M gross profit
4. **R&D Investment**: $1.2M in R&D shows innovation focus
5. **Customer Concentration**: 10 customers with average $525K contract value
6. **Product Performance**: AI Analytics Platform is top performer ($2.25M revenue)

### Recommendations the AI Should Suggest
1. **Cost Optimization**: Reduce operating expenses by 15-20%
2. **Pricing Strategy**: Consider price increases for high-demand products
3. **Customer Expansion**: Diversify customer base to reduce concentration risk
4. **Efficiency Improvements**: Streamline operations to improve margins
5. **Cash Management**: Leverage strong cash position for strategic investments
